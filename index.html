ZerPhone

The flower industry in Israel is estimated in 950 million NIS in 2011. Therefor ZerPhone is an online app/web site for ordering flower and gifts, from anywhere, anytime.

Why Us?

Our key strength lies in that we give an easy platform for flower stores owners on selling their product. Most of the flower stores owners are not technical personas and missing on a huge market share. Currently this market share is almost 100% Zer4U business. With the combination of many small stores that are spread out all over the country, the product range and diversity will be large then zer4u, because they only sell their own products.

Features

Must:

Show all the products available Making a purchase Showing all stores Admin control One click purchase Ads Stay signed in

Nice to have:

Facebook/google+ sign in Comments on products

Future:

Navigate from current location to the nearest store. Suggestions for products based on previous purchases. Social platforms integration.
